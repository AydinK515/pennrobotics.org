FIRST Robotics
Penn-Harris-Madison Schools provide FIRST Robotics programs for students from elementary to high school.
FRC
FIRST Robotics Competiton is offered for junior and senior high school students. Students will be faced with an ultimate challenge, experience how to build and program an industrial-size robot, raise funds, and much more.
FTC
FIRST Tech Challenge is offered for freshmen and sophomores high school students. Students will design, build, program, and control robots to compete in a challenge.
FLL
FIRST Lego League is offered for elementary and middle school students. Students learn problem-solving skills, engage in hands-on STEM experiences, and completes challenges using LEGOs.
