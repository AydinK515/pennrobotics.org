Junior STEM Camps

Junior Stem is a Penn-Harris-Madison program for students in K-5th grade. The goal of the program is to develop technical and scientific knowledge while strengthening teamwork and problem-solving skills.

Fall/Winter Workshop

We recognize that our youth STEM families are often looking for activities during the school year, so we created biweekly workshops during November and December!

Summer Camps
Not only do we provide students with a unique one-on-one, hands-on learning experience, but we make sure your kids have fun in the process! Coopertition (cooperation and competition) is a large part of robotics at the high school level, and we make sure we bring that same experience to your kids when they enroll in any of our Penn Robotics Youth Camps. Our summer camps occur annually in June and sign-ups typically open around March.
